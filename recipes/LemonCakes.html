Sansa's Lemon Cakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup + 2 tbs suagr
  • 2 eggs + 1 egg yolk
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 or 3 Meyer lemons

Steps

  1. Slice Meyer lemons into 12 thin circles.
  2. In a sauce pot, simmer water and sugar. Place the lemon slices in the pot and simmer for five minutes
  3. Remove the slices from the sauce pot and dry overnight on greased parchment paper.
  1. Preheat oven to 325°F.
  2. Grease a muffin tray and then lay a candied lemon slice in each muffin cup.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, whisk together eggs, yolk, sugar, lemon zest, lemon juice, vanilla extract, and sour cream.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, and salt. Gently fold in the wet mixture and then fold in melted butter.
  5. Scoop about 1½ ounces of batter into each muffin cup. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes.
  6. Remove from the oven and immediately flip onto a parchment lined baking sheet. Allow the cakes to cool for 5 minutes in the tray and then remove.
  7. Now, enjoy!
